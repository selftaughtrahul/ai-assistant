# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Executive Summary
The AI-Powered Customer Support Chatbot aims to reduce the workload of human support agents by at least 40% by automating responses to frequently asked questions and common workflows (e.g., order tracking for e-commerce, leave policy queries for HR).

## 2. Business Objectives
- **Cost Reduction:** Decrease operational costs associated with tier-1 customer support.
- **24/7 Availability:** Provide round-the-clock support to clients and internal employees.
- **Improved Resolution Time:** Instantly resolve common queries without human intervention.
- **Seamless Handoff:** Ensure high-value or complex problems are seamlessly escalated to human agents without losing context.

## 3. Target Audience
- **External Customers:** E-commerce shoppers needing help with orders, returns, and product info.
- **Internal Employees:** HR personnel needing basic info on payroll, policies, and IT support.
- **Human Support Agents:** Will receive escalated chats with complete contextual history.

## 4. Key Business Features
- **Natural Language Understanding:** Ability to process unstructured text inputted by users to understand their intent.
- **Conversational Awareness (Context):** System should remember past interactions within the same session.
- **Intelligent Routing (Fallback):** Recognizing when the bot cannot resolve an issue and routing it to the appropriate human department.

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Deflection Rate:** Percentage of conversations resolved without human intervention (Target: 40%+).
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** User ratings post-interaction for bot and human-escalated chats.
- **Latency:** Average response time per query (Target: <1s backend processing).
